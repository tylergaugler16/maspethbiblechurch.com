Open Gym

Open gym will resume on September 19th . Please note that we only have space for kids that are on our mailing list from Open Gym or our summer programs. If you were on the waiting list last year, you will also be accepted if you still want to come. Siblings of kids already on our list may also come. If any additional children want to join they will be added to our wait list. Please register them early so there is a chance they will get in this year. There is NO registration fee this year, but you do have to fill out the registration form coming in your flyer. If you are NOT planning to attend, please let us know. Remember if you miss 6 meetings in a row, you are put on the waiting list.
